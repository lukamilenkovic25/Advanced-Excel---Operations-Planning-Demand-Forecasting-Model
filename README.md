# Advanced_Excel-Operations_Planning_&_Demand_Forecasting_Model
## Project Overview
This project showcases an advanced Excel‑based operations planning and demand forecasting model, designed for business users who rely on Excel for operational and scenario‑driven decision‑making. The solution combines structured assumptions, dynamic forecasting logic, capacity constraints, and scenario analysis to support realistic planning under uncertainty.

## Business Objective
- Forecast demand across multiple regions
- Assess the impact of growth, seasonality, and capacity limits
- Compare Base, Optimistic, and Pessimistic scenarios
- Quantify revenue upside and downside risks
- Deliver a transparent, Excel‑native planning solution
  
---

## Model Structure
  - **Raw_Data** – historical demand and pricing data (monthly, multi‑region
  - **Assumptions** – scenario selection, growth rates, seasonality factors, capacity limits, and price adjustments
  - **Forecast_Model** – dynamic forecast calculations incorporating business assumptions and constraints
  - **Scenario_Analysis** – monthly aggregation and scenario comparison using snapshot logic
  - **Dashboard** – executive‑level view of revenue forecasts and scenario outcomes
 
---

## Key Techniques & Features
- Advanced Excel formulas (XLOOKUP, SWITCH, SUMIFS, EOMONTH, MIN)
- Scenario‑driven planning without VBA or Power Pivot
- Capacity‑constrained demand forecasting
- Clean separation between data, logic, and visualization layers
- Business‑focused Excel dashboard design
 
---

## Profit & Pricing Assumptions
Revenue impact is calculated using an adjustable price change parameter, with 0% price adjustment used as the baseline. This enables controlled what‑if analysis without altering underlying demand logic.
